Leaflet Example

Here's a simple example of clustering using the Leaflet library. Whole page can be customized and personalized to the needs of the project, including the legend and layer list with explanations etc. This data represents all the companies from the FAME database, not yet categorised. Number in the cluster circle indicates the number of the businesses in the area of the cluster, zooming in will display the exact position of the company on the map and clicking on the pin will display the pop-up info about the company

Whole page can be customized and attached to the Github page of the project (which is a part of the scope of the project).
